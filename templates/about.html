What It Is

Football Forecaster is an predicive analytic tool that utilizes college football efficiency stats to determine the outcome of a matchup between a home and away team. A user simply selects two teams and the app will provide a probability of victory outcome.

The Algorithm

Our machine learning program utilizes a multivirate non-linear (logistic) regression analysis that has been trained on college football efficency stats from the 2008-2016 season. The algorith intakes the following stats:

  • Offensive net passing yards per attempt
  • Offensive rushing yds per att
  • Offensive interceptions per att
  • Offensive fumbles per play
  • Defensive net passing yds per att
  • Defensive net passing yds per att
  • Defensive rushing yds per att
  • Team penalty yds per play
  • Home field advantage
